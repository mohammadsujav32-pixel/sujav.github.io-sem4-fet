About FreeCodeCamp

FreeCodeCamp (also referred to as Free Code Camp) is a non-profit educational organization that consists of an interactive learning web platform, an online community forum, chat rooms, online publications, and local organizations that make learning software development and computer programming accessible to anyone.

Beginning with tutorials that introduce students to HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, C#, etc., students progress to project assignments that they complete either alone or in pairs.
